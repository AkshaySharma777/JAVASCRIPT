OPERATORS

Javascript operators are symbols that are used to perform operations on operands. 

        1.  Arithemtic Operators => Addition +, Subtraction -, Multiplication -, Division /, Modulus %, Increment ++, Decrement --

        2.  Comparison (Relational) Operators => Is equal to ==, Not equal to !=, Not Identical !==, Greater than >, Greater equal to >=, Lesser than <, Less than or equal to <=

        3.  Bitwise Operators => Bitwise AND &, Bitwise OR |, Bitwise NOT ~, Bitwise XOR ^, Bitwise Left Shift <<, Bitwise Right Shift withero >>>,

        4. Logical Operators => Logical AND &&, Logical OR ||, Logical NOT ! 

        5.  Assignment Operators => Assign =, Add and Assign +=, Subtract and Assign -=, Multiply and Assign *=, Divide and Assign /=, Modulus and Assign %=

        6. Special or Ternary Operators => condition ? "true":"false";
